About Café Canada

Café Canada is a proudly Canadian initiative dedicated to providing high-quality, locally sourced coffee and food to communities across the nation. Established in 2020, we have grown from a single location in Ottawa to over 50 locations in every province and territory.

Our journey began with a simple idea: create a space where Canadians could gather to enjoy exceptional coffee while supporting local farmers and producers. Every bean we serve is sourced from Canadian-partnered farms committed to sustainable practices.

We believe in the power of community. That is why we reinvest 5% of our profits into local food banks and community programs. We also provide employment and training opportunities for newcomers to Canada, helping them build new lives in their adopted home.

Our values

Sustainability

We use compostable cups and lids, source local ingredients whenever possible, and power our roasting facility with renewable energy.

Inclusion

Our hiring practices prioritize diversity. Over 40% of our staff are newcomers to Canada, and we provide language training and career development programs.

Notre mission : Servir un café d'exception tout en bâtissant des communautés fortes et inclusives à travers le Canada. Chaque tasse raconte une histoire de collaboration, de durabilité et de fierté canadienne.
